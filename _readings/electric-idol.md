## Hook & thesis
Katee Robert's *Electric Idol* electrifies the contemporary mythos of Eros and Psyche, transforming their passion into a visceral exploration of love's darker impulses. **Verdict:** This novel is a masterclass in blending desire with danger, compelling readers to confront the seductive pull of obsession and power.

## The contextual pivot
Nestled within the burgeoning subgenre of dark romance, *Electric Idol* occupies a unique space by marrying contemporary retellings with mythological roots. Robert, known for her ability to weave intricate emotional landscapes, draws readers who seek both the familiar and the provocative. The audience here is likely familiar with the tropes of forbidden romance and power dynamics, yet Robert's rendition invites a broader readership, bridging niche enthusiasts with those craving an exhilarating plunge into myth reimagined. The tone is unmistakably gothic, setting it apart from lighter fare, and anchoring it within the darker recesses of the romance genre. 

## Deep-dive critique
The structure of *Electric Idol* is deftly paced, allowing for a gradual build of tension that mirrors the characters' tumultuous relationship. The dual perspective — alternating between Eros and Psyche — enhances the emotional stakes, offering a window into their conflicting desires and vulnerabilities. Robert’s prose is laden with a lushness that complements the electric theme; her dialogue snaps with an intensity that elevates the stakes, making every interaction pulse with urgency. 

However, the thematic success lies not just in the palpable heat but also in the exploration of autonomy versus manipulation. Eros, as a character, embodies the archetype of the charming yet dangerous lover, while Psyche's journey challenges the notion of agency within a relationship steeped in power dynamics. Their union is both a dance of passion and a struggle for self-identity, culminating in an ending that feels both exhilarating and earned — a testament to Robert's understanding of her characters' complexities. Yet, at times, the pacing falters under the weight of exposition, slightly dulling the sharpness of the narrative arc. 

## Discussion launchpad
*Electric Idol* raises questions of desire, autonomy, and the boundaries of love, making it fertile ground for discussion. The inherent tension between passion and peril creates polarizing views among readers regarding the portrayal of relationships that toe the line between toxic and transformative.
