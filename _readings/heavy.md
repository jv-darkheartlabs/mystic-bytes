## Hook & thesis
In M. L. Burns' *Heavy*, we delve into the treacherous waters of forbidden love that illustrates how desire can morph into obsession, ensnaring both the heart and the mind in a darkly poetic embrace. **Verdict:** This book masterfully intertwines the seductive allure of taboo romance with a haunting exploration of the human psyche, leaving readers breathless and questioning the very nature of love itself.

## The contextual pivot
*Heavy* occupies a precarious niche within the forbidden love genre, a space often overshadowed by more conventional romances. Burns' work aligns with contemporary authors like Anna Zaires and Tarryn Fisher, who similarly explore dark themes and moral ambiguity. Yet, Burns transcends mere titillation — she crafts a narrative that bridges niche tropes to reach a broader audience, appealing to readers who crave not just heat but depth. The invisible audience here includes those drawn to psychological complexities and the darker sides of human connection, as the narrative challenges societal norms while retaining a literary flair.

## Deep-dive critique
Structurally, *Heavy* unfolds with a deliberate pacing that mirrors the suffocating tension between the protagonists. Burns employs a close third-person point of view, allowing readers intimate access to the characters’ tumultuous thoughts and desires, which amplifies the themes of obsession and longing. The prose itself is lush and evocative, with imagery that intertwines death and desire, particularly effective in scenes laden with foreboding. For example, the recurring motif of shadows lurking in the background serves not only as a visual cue but also as a metaphor for the characters' internal struggles.

Characterization shines through the duality of the protagonists: they are both deeply flawed and achingly relatable, drawing readers into their moral quagmire. Dialogue is sharp and filled with subtext, reflecting the characters' unspoken fears and desires, though at times it may verge on overwrought. The narrative’s climactic moments, while intensely charged, feel earned due to the careful build-up of emotional stakes throughout the story. However, the ending could polarize readers; some may find it profoundly satisfying, while others could argue it lacks resolution, leaving critical questions about the characters' futures hanging in the balance.

## Discussion launchpad
*Heavy* stirs a volatile mix of emotions and ethical dilemmas that will likely fuel heated discussions. It prompts us to reflect on the limits of love and the consequences of crossing societal boundaries. 

**Questions for the room:**  
- Does the portrayal of obsession as romantic enhance or undermine the narrative's thematic depth?  
- How does the author balance the seductive elements of the plot with the darker implications of the characters' choices?  
- What are the implications of the unresolved ending — does it elevate or diminish the overall experience?  
- In what ways does *Heavy* challenge or conform to traditional views of love and morality?  
- How do the craft choices influence your emotional response to the characters and their fates?
