## Hook & thesis
In *Zodiac Academy: Restless Stars*, Caroline Peckham and Susanne Valenti confront the tumultuous dance of power and vulnerability within a world rife with supernatural hierarchies and dark secrets. **Verdict:** This installation of the series masterfully escalates emotional stakes while ensnaring the reader in a web of desire and conflict that is both intoxicating and perilous.

## The contextual pivot
As a pivotal entry in the bully-romance genre, *Restless Stars* expertly navigates familiar tropes of rivalry and forbidden attraction, yet it offers a deeper exploration of trauma and healing. Peckham and Valenti have cultivated a niche audience that thrives on the interplay between celestial motifs and the harsh realities of interpersonal conflict, bridging the gap to more general readers through their blend of fantasy and raw emotionality. Their work stands alongside similar series such as *Cruel Prince* by Holly Black, where the allure of power dynamics and dark romance compels readers to question the nature of love and loyalty.

## Deep-dive critique
Crafted with gripping tension, Peckham and Valenti employ dual perspectives to enhance the emotional complexity of their characters, allowing readers to experience the conflicting motivations driving their actions. The pacing is particularly effective, with the narrative oscillating between heart-racing confrontations and intimate moments that expose the vulnerabilities cloaked beneath bravado. The world-building is steeped in astrological lore, lending a rich symbolic framework that underscores the protagonists' journeys.

However, the dialogue occasionally falters, veering into melodrama that can distract from the deeper themes of grief and belonging. While the explicit content is designed to evoke a visceral response, it sometimes overshadows the character development, leaving moments of potential vulnerability overshadowed by physical encounters. Despite these missteps, the emotional climax of the book is well-earned, weaving together the threads of conflict, desire, and resolution in a way that feels satisfying yet leaves the door ajar for future explorations.

## Discussion launchpad
The novel presents a tantalizing dichotomy between the intoxicating thrill of power and the perilous consequences of vulnerability. This tension can ignite passionate debates among readers.

**Questions for the room:**  
- How effectively do the authors balance explicit content with emotional depth?  
- In what ways do power dynamics in this book reflect real-world relationships?  
- Does the dual perspective enhance or detract from the story's emotional impact?  
- How do the themes of grief and trauma resonate with the romance elements in the narrative?  
- Can the resolution be seen as genuinely redemptive, or does it perpetuate cycles of toxicity?
