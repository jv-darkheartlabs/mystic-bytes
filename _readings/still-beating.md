## Hook & thesis
In *Still Beating*, Jennifer Hartmann plunges readers into a harrowing emotional abyss where the lines between captor and captive blur with devastating consequences. **Verdict:** This novel masterfully dissects the complexities of desire and trauma, crafting an unnervingly intimate portrait of survival that lingers long after the last page.

## The contextual pivot
Hartmann operates within the dark romance subgenre, a realm often fraught with moral ambiguity and emotional turbulence. Like the works of Anna Zaires and Talia Hibbert, *Still Beating* navigates the treacherous waters of captivity while simultaneously exploring the depths of human connection. However, Hartmann's approach is distinctly literary, favoring psychological nuance over sensationalism, which allows her to bridge the gap between niche dark romance readers and a broader audience seeking deeper emotional resonance. The story's themes of trauma and resilience are not merely plot devices; they act as conduits for a profound exploration of what it means to endure in the face of relentless despair.

## Deep-dive critique
The narrative structure of *Still Beating* employs a nonlinear timeline, artfully juxtaposing past and present to illuminate the protagonist’s psychological journey. This choice enhances the tension, as memories of her past encroach upon her present reality, creating a palpable sense of urgency. Hartmann’s use of first-person perspective allows for a raw and intimate connection with the protagonist, immersing readers in her emotional landscape. The prose is both haunting and evocative, painting vivid imagery that encapsulates the duality of fear and longing. 

Characterization is another of Hartmann's strengths; the development of the captor is particularly compelling, revealing layers of vulnerability beneath the veneer of menace. Dialogues are sharp and laden with subtext, revealing unspoken desires and the intricate dance of power dynamics at play. However, while the emotional depth is commendable, some readers may find that the pacing occasionally falters, particularly in scenes that linger too long on introspection and disrupt the narrative momentum. Still, the ending, while challenging, feels earned — a cathartic release that highlights the possibility of healing amid chaos.

## Discussion launchpad
*Still Beating* is bound to spark intense discussions on the morality of its themes and the portrayal of trauma. Its exploration of desire within captivity raises questions about consent and agency, polarizing readers who may find themselves grappling with the ethical implications of such a narrative. 

**Questions for the room:**  
- Does the portrayal of trauma in the novel risk romanticizing captivity, or does it offer a necessary exploration of survival?  
- How does Hartmann's writing style influence our perception of the protagonists' emotional states?  
- In what ways does the duality of captor and captive reflect larger societal power dynamics?  
- Can we reconcile empathy for the captor with the horror of their actions, or does it remain an irreconcilable conflict?
