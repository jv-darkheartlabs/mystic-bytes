## Hook & thesis
Katee Robert's *Desperate Measures* plunges the reader into a world where power dynamics and desire intertwine, crafting a dark tapestry of love born from animosity. **Verdict:** This modern retelling of a classic myth robustly challenges the boundaries of consent and attraction, leaving readers breathless yet conflicted.

## The contextual pivot
Nestled within the enemies-to-lovers trope, *Desperate Measures* stands as a provocative entry in the realm of dark romance. Robert deftly plays with archetypes, drawing from the well-trodden paths of villainous seduction while infusing fresh energy into the narrative. This work not only captivates the niche audience that adores wicked villains but also invites general readers to reconsider their preconceptions of love and power. In the landscape of Robert's body of work, this novel exemplifies her signature style — a blend of erotic tension and emotional depth, extending the conversation on morality in romance. 

## Deep-dive critique
The structure of *Desperate Measures* is a masterclass in pacing; Robert maintains a relentless momentum that mirrors the protagonists' escalating desires. The dual POV allows readers to navigate the murky waters of their tumultuous relationship, though at times it skews toward predictability, risking the tension cultivated in earlier chapters. Dialogue crackles with a sharpness that reveals character motivations, yet there are moments where the prose could benefit from a deeper exploration of emotional stakes. 

Thematically, the novel wrestles with the notion of power imbalance, cleverly utilizing the mythological backdrop to examine the complex dance of consent. Robert's characters are not mere caricatures; they are flawed individuals grappling with their darker impulses. However, the moral ambiguity may polarize readers, provoking questions about empathy and complicity in romantic entanglements. The resolution, while provocative, may leave some unmet expectations, depending on one’s tolerance for ambiguity in romantic conclusions.

## Discussion launchpad
*Desperate Measures* serves as fertile ground for discourse, with its bold exploration of consent and desire sure to ignite passionate debate. It raises questions about the nature of love when ensnared in a web of manipulation and allure.

- How does Robert's use of myth enhance or detract from the contemporary themes of the narrative?  
- What role does moral ambiguity play in your enjoyment of dark romance?
