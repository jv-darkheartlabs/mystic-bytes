Remote work on the road is calendar math plus honest boundaries. The view does not replace standups, async updates, or sleep.

**Focus:** How to Build a Flexible Work Schedule While.

Block overlap hours deliberately. Document async decisions. Protect sleep when meetings cross midnight. Remote work while traveling fails when you treat every city like a vacation and every day like an office day — pick a rhythm per base and hold it for at least two weeks before you judge it.

— JV · Dark Heart Labs.
