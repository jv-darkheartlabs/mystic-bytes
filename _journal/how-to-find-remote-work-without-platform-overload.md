Remote work on the road is calendar math plus honest boundaries. The view does not replace standups, async updates, or sleep.

**Focus:** finding roles that fit your stack and timezone reality.

Pick two platforms maximum — one generalist board, one community or niche channel you actually check weekly. Filter by timezone overlap with Oceania if that is your base. Ignore listings that say "remote worldwide" but schedule daily standups at 2 AM your time.

Portfolio beats volume: one repo with README, one writing sample, one sentence on how you communicate async. Apply fewer places with tailored messages.

Remote search is a slow pipeline. Treat it like a job, not a weekend binge.

— JV · Dark Heart Labs.
