Wellness for builders is not luxury — it is maintenance. Scent, touch, and ritual can support recovery when used with realistic expectations. Nothing here replaces medical care.

Photoaging is UV-driven. Retinoids and sunscreen are evidence-backed; oils may support barrier comfort but do not reverse wrinkles magically. Sleep and hydration help appearance because biology, not marketing.
