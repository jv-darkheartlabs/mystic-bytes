Wellness for builders is not luxury — it is maintenance. Scent, touch, and ritual can support recovery when used with realistic expectations. Nothing here replaces medical care.

Wearables that puff lavender exist. Ask: does this solve a problem or add charging cables? Useful: timed diffuser with quiet motor. Gimmick: app-controlled mood 'algorithm' with no evidence.
