Wellness for builders is not luxury — it is maintenance. Scent, touch, and ritual can support recovery when used with realistic expectations. Nothing here replaces medical care.

Developers live in visual and auditory load. Scent can mark state change when willpower is low — start work, stop work, sleep. See [sensory load as a system resource](/p/sensory-load-is-a-system-resource/). Tech cannot smell; you can use low-tech scent intentionally.
