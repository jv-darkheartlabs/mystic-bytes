Wellness for builders is not luxury — it is maintenance. Scent, touch, and ritual can support recovery when used with realistic expectations. Nothing here replaces medical care.

**Can I ingest?** Generally no without professional supervision. **How dilute?** Typical adult topical: 1–2% for face, 2–3% body. **Are all oils equal?** No — look for botanical name, batch, GC/MS if available. **Pets?** Many oils are toxic to cats; research before diffusing.

— JV · Dark Heart Labs.
