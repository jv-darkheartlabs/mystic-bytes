Wellness for builders is not luxury — it is maintenance. Scent, touch, and ritual can support recovery when used with realistic expectations. Nothing here replaces medical care.

AC and monitor heat dry skin. Hydrate, use non-comedogenic moisturizer, clean pillowcase. Essential oils like tea tree may support some people in diluted spot treatment — patch-test; can irritate. See a dermatologist for persistent acne.
